Curry Shrimp With Vegetables

by Jerome Scott Updated on 10/11/2022

TIME:

  • Prep: 10 mins
  • Cook: 10 mins
  • Total: 20 mins
  • Servings: 2 to 4 servings

    • NUTRITION:

  • 252 Calories
  • 14g
  • Fat
  • 9g Carbs
  • 21g Protein

    • Curry paste adds flavor to carrot, peas, and red bell pepper in this easy curry shrimp recipe that takes less than 20 minutes to make. This stir-fry pairs perfectly with rice or noodles.

    Ingredients

    • 3/4 pound fresh medium shrimp

    • 1 large carrot

    • 1 red bell pepper

    • 6 ounces snow peas

    • 3 1/2 tablespoons oil, or as needed, divided

    • 1 teaspoon minced ginger, about 2 thin slices

    • 1 tablespoon curry paste

    • 1 tablespoon Chinese rice wine, or dry sherry

    • 1/4 teaspoon sugar, or to taste

    • 1Soak the shrimp in warm salted water for 5 minutes. Rinse in cold water, drain and pat dry with paper towels. Shell, devein and chop.

    • 2Peel and slice the carrot into 1/2-inch slices on the diagonal. Cut the red bell pepper in half, remove the seeds and chop into chunks.

    • 3Soak the shrimp in warm salted water for 5 minutes. Rinse in cold water, drain and pat dry with paper towels. Shell, devein and chop.

    • 4 Peel and slice the carrot into 1/2-inch slices on the diagonal. Cut the red bell pepper in half, remove the seeds and chop into chunks.

    • 5In a large pot filled with boiling water, briefly blanch (parboil) the peas and carrots until the peas turn bright green, and the vegetables are crisp but still tender. Remove, plunge briefly into ice cold water, and drain thoroughly.

    • 6tablespoons oil to the heated wok. Add the ginger and stir-fry briefly until aromatic (about 30 seconds). Add the shrimp. Stir-fry until they turn pink. Remove the shrimp from the pan.

    • 7 Heat 1 1/2 tablespoons oil to the wok. When the oil is hot, add the curry paste. Heat briefly until it is aromatic (30 seconds to 1 minute). Add the carrots. Stir-fry for 1 minute, then add the red bell pepper. Add the peas. Stir-fry

    • 8briefly, then add the shrimp back into the pan. Mix everything together and cook for 1 minute, then add the rice wine or sherry and the sugar. Cook for a bit longer and serve hot.
